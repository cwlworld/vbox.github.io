About me

本人於2018年畢業於台灣科技大學設計所,我的研究題目為待辦事項行動應用軟體的使用者體驗分析。一直以來不管是所學或從事的工作皆與設計相關,設計是一門很有趣的學科,其牽涉的範圍也極為廣泛,只要我們在生活中多一點留心，大大小小的發現都能成為設計的亮點。

在從事設計工作中，主要負責圖像的設計、包裝設計、產品說明書的編輯、展場設計規劃、行銷設計文宣的製作、產品造型配色討論等，除了設計相關的工作外，也需要與工廠或供應商溝通檢討材質與製程方式，跟供應商交流的過程中可以學習到很多設計以外的知識。當然工作內容不免會有一些枯燥的地方，但我會把它當作是一種自我耐心的訓練，即便是繁瑣的工作也必須要嚴謹的去完成，才能讓最後的成果有完美的呈現。不會將自己局限在某一類的設計，希望能夠多元的發展。

設計的工作在於改善與創新，將概念具體化，將訊息圖像化，目的就是要利於使用者並為公司帶來營收，這也是我努力的目標。最後希望能運用自身的專業與經驗來為貴公司服務,並與大家互相學習。

About me

I graduated from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology of design department in 2018. My research is about user experience field. I study and work in the design field in whole my life. I thought it is an interesting subject and it related to the wide category. We pay attention to things in life that we always can find something to improve.

As I work in design job, my responsibilities include graphic design, package design, user manual edit, booth design, marketing material design, product modeling and color plan etc. Otherwise, I need to communicate with factory or supplier about material and manufacturability problems. It is a good opportunity to know new knowledge beside design. Sometimes I have to do boring work that I will treat it like some kinds of patient practice. We need to pay attention to details that make elegance. I am willing to access different design fields, be a multi-designer.

The main idea of design is improvement and innovation. It is the translation from concept to substance, from information to graphic. The purpose is to bring benefit to the user and bring income to the company. That is what I am pursuing. I hope my skill、knowledge、experience that meet your needs. Look forward to working with you to create a better future.
